How we work

You have the idea, we have the solution
Preliminary studies & requirements
Through workshops, we go through your ideas and visions. Then rewrites them into requirements specification and creates wireframes.
Design & concept
Using the information from the workshop, we create design sketches for your project based on wireframes and your wishes.
Development
Once you have approved the design sketch, we begin development. With the help of our project tools, you also receive ongoing updates throughout the course of the project.
Launch
When everything is finished and the final tests have been completed, it's time to launch! Here you get a complete picture of your project and it is from here that we start working with growth.
Ongoing growth
We work together with you to create growth and increase the profitability of your company. By working data-driven, we can assist with our insights into how you should think to maximize your growth and your online presence.
